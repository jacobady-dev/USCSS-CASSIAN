WILSON'S AGENDA ACT II.
The discoveries on the Cronus are beyond your wildest dreams. It's dangerous to be sure, but if you pull this off and manage to salvage the xenomorphic material you can parlay it into a fortune, even a promotion. No matter what, Special Order 966 is your goal—but don't risk open confrontation with the rest of the Cassian crew. You still need them (for now...).
