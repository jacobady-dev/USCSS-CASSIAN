GM NOTES
Hand each player their banter card at the start of the session.

Tell everyone they’re waking up from hypersleep and are groggy as hell. Then each player should take turns reading or paraphrasing a line from their banter card.

Let the scene continue until the table has naturally surfaced the cargo, Sutter’s World, the sensor glitch, the Daisy, money pressure, and the crew dynamics.

Encourage them to improvise and make it organic.
